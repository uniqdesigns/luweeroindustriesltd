LUWERO INDUSTRIES LTD

Luwero Industries Limited

Is a subsidiary of NEC(National Enterprise Corporation) and is legally incorporated and funded by the government of Uganda with existing partnerships from various development partners and Investors. We are currently engaged in different forms of businesses cutting across manufacturing,construction, modern Agriculture, farming and engineering.

National Enterprise Corporation

National Enterprise Corporation was established in 1989 by the Act of Parliament to serve as a commercial arm of the Ministry of Defence/ Uganda People’s Defence Forces and to produce goods and services that are beneficial to the Defence Forces and the general public. Its commercial ventures are undertaken through its Subsidiaries and Joint Ventures.

We exist to partner with businesses to develop, grow and sustain value in the environment that is still grappling with challenges of inadequacies of investment capital, infrastructure deficiencies, wide cultural variations and other circumstances peculiar to Africa.

WHAT WE DO

We have acquired state of the art machinery for production and repair of military Equipment through joint venture programs with established Companies across the Globe.

There are exciting opportunities for Government Entities and the private sector to do business directly with Luwero Industries and indirectly with our major partners at sub-contract level. The programmes we support are detailed in the Defence Equipment Plan; these include equipment for defence and security agencies. We also do general manufacturing of public consumer goods as well as building and construction for government and private sector. Our Service include;

  • Production of bullet proof vests and helmets.
  • Production of caliber and 40 x 46mm Grenade.
  • Repair and reconditioning of small calibre guns.
  • Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul (MRO) of Mi series helicopters.
  • Production of Emulsion explosives and Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oils (ANFO).

Production of bullet proof vests and helmets
Luwero Industries Limited in partnership with BOSASY produces high level bullet proof helmets and vests like Pol-28, Beta-32 and Alfa-34 Extra long. The Body armor produced by Luwero Industries have got 5 to 10 years of warranty. The vests are designed to keep their protective qualities for the specified period. Most body armors worn everyday keep losing the protective capability. Our bullet proofs last for the exact time specified in our sales agreement.

Production of caliber and 40 x 46mm Grenade
Luwero Industries Limited produces ammunition of calibre 7.62 x 39MM for AK-47,SMG, KMG and SAR. Our ammunition products are tried and tested and can match the required standards at global level. We also produce low velocity armour piercing anti personnel 40 x 46mm grenades. We are mindful of quality and our main focus is capturing the market across the great lakes region and the rest of Africa. We can’t ignore compelling value that we offer our target market.

Repair and reconditioning of small calibre guns
We repair and recondition small fire calibre guns and fire arms for the Uganda Peoples’ Defence Forces, all government security agencies and private security firms licensed by Uganda Police. Fire arms which do not operate properly due to broken parts, worn out or misssing parts can be diagonised and put back to proper working order. We believe our brand is in many ways synonymous with our reputation as a Company. It refers to the way our clients see us.

Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul (MRO) of Mi series helicopters
NEC Pro Heli is a joint venture for LIL for Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul (MRO) of Mi series helicopters. This will go a long way in improving the capabilities of the UPDF , reducing overall maintenance costs for the MI-17/24 helicopters as well as achieving a local industrial base and self-sustenance. Pro Heli’s partnership is between Ministry of Defence and Veterans Affairs together with Bosasy Uganda Limited.
